Policy

Privacy policy.

How information submitted through this website is handled in a simple service inquiry context.

Information collected

This site may collect details you choose to submit, such as your name, email address, phone number, service interest, location, timeline, and project notes.

How information is used

Inquiry details are used to respond to your message, discuss project scope, coordinate booking logistics, and provide relevant studio updates when you opt in.

Newsletter

If you join the mailing list, your email is used for studio updates, booking windows, portfolio news, and related announcements. You can request removal at any time.

Sharing information

Personal inquiry details are not sold. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the website, communicate with you, or comply with lawful requirements.

Contact

For privacy questions or update requests, contact the studio through the contact page.
