Policy

Cancellations and rescheduling.

A plain-language policy page for timing changes, weather, access issues, and session coordination.

Rescheduling

If timing needs to change, contact the studio as early as possible. New dates depend on availability, location, and project requirements.

Weather or access issues

Outdoor property images, exterior details, and location-based shoots may need adjustment for weather, site access, safety, or unexpected conditions.

Client readiness

Locations, dishes, spaces, or products should be ready at the confirmed session time. Delays may affect the final shot list or require a new session window.

Confirmed agreements

Any project-specific cancellation, rescheduling, deposit, or licensing terms should be confirmed directly as part of the booking agreement.

Questions

For questions about a scheduled project, use the contact page and include the project name, location, and target date.
