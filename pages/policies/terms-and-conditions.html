Terms and conditions

A clear guide to inquiries, bookings, privacy, and cancellations.

These terms explain how MoranSha Photography handles website inquiries, project planning, client information, scheduling changes, and creative work.

Before a project is booked

Submitting an inquiry form starts a conversation, but it does not automatically confirm a shoot. A booking is confirmed only after the project scope, date, location, deliverables, timeline, and any required agreement details are reviewed and accepted by both sides.

Project details and creative work

Each project is planned around the intended use of the images, the shoot location, the subject matter, and the final delivery needs. Usage rights, licensing, editing expectations, delivery timing, and any special requirements should be confirmed before work begins.

Client readiness

Locations, dishes, properties, products, people, access instructions, and creative references should be ready at the confirmed session time. If something important is delayed or unavailable, the final shot list, session length, or delivery timeline may need to change.

Rescheduling and cancellations

If a session needs to move, please contact the studio as early as possible. New dates depend on availability, project needs, location access, and weather. Any project-specific deposit, cancellation, or rescheduling terms should be confirmed as part of the booking agreement.

Weather, access, and safety

Outdoor shoots, real estate exteriors, drone-style visuals, and location-based projects may need adjustment because of weather, site access, safety, building rules, or unexpected conditions. The goal is always to protect the quality of the work and keep the shoot practical.

How your information is used

Information you submit through the website, such as your name, email address, phone number, service interest, location, timeline, and project notes, is used to respond to your inquiry, discuss project scope, coordinate booking logistics, and communicate with you about your request.

Privacy

Your personal inquiry details are not sold. Information may be shared only when needed to operate the website, communicate with you, support the project, or comply with lawful requirements. If you join any mailing list or update list, you can request removal at any time.

Website content

The text, layout, visuals, and original project materials on this website may not be reused without permission. Website content is provided for general information and may change as services, availability, and studio details evolve.

Questions

If you have questions about a project, a scheduled session, privacy, or these terms, please use the contact page and include the project name, location, target date, and any details that would help the studio respond clearly.
