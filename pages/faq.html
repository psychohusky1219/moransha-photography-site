FAQ

Questions before booking.

A quick guide to timelines, planning, image delivery, locations, and what to prepare before your session.

How do I start a booking?

Send a project inquiry through the contact page with the service type, location, timing, and what the images will be used for.

What should I prepare before a food shoot?

Prepare a shot list, menu priorities, available surfaces, plating needs, props, and any visual references that match your brand direction.

What should be ready for real estate photography?

Spaces should be clean, decluttered, and shoot-ready before arrival. Open blinds, hide personal items, and make sure key rooms are accessible.

How are final images delivered?

Edited images are delivered digitally in a format suited to your intended use, such as web, social, listings, menus, or campaign materials.

Can you help with creative direction?

Yes. The booking process includes alignment on mood, usage, priorities, and practical styling notes before the session.

Do you travel for shoots?

Location needs are discussed during booking. Share the address or city in the inquiry so timing and logistics can be confirmed.
